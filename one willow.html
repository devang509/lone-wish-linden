How to Cast Your Wish

1

Find the Linden

Close your eyes and breathe in the scent — wet earth, green bark, and the faintest trace of moonlight. When you can feel the linden's presence, you're ready.

2

Speak Your Wish

Whisper it to the leaves. The linden listens only to truths spoken from the heart — not wants of the mind, but needs of the soul.

3

Release It to the Wind

Let go. The wish is no longer yours — it belongs to the linden now. Watch as it rises through the branches, becoming one with the night sky.

4

Live in Expectation

The linden never forgets. Walk forward with quiet certainty, and one day you'll realize — the world has reshaped itself around your wish.
